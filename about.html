Hatamoto Knife Co. is a Japan company without foreign capital. Our business is concerned, in particular, with the development, design and the subsequent sale of name brand knives and tools.

The registered office of Hatamoto Knife Co. is situated in Itabashi, Tokyo an industrial centre with a long entrepreneurial tradition that was developed from the second half of the 20 th century.
